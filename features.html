Powerful And Flexible Form Builder

Loyica form builder provides the flexibility to build completely custom forms to capture the important information from your customers; divide your form into different steps, add hidden fields to be captured from URL, Cookies or POST parameters, dynamic forms based on conditions, all these are built in features.

Set entry points for your business, custom contact forms, custom application forms, campaign forms and any type of form can be built with Loyica Form Builder.

Loyica form builder features a wide range of standard input types (e.g. text , email, number, date) as well as advanced input types (e.g. Hidden fields, Country, Formulas), all these to create the perfect forms for you.

Loyica forms can be intelligent too, set conditions to show or hide certain fields based on your criteria, add score to the clients answers to further integrate your business logic into the system.
